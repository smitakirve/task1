Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese

Instructions

Step 1: Boil water in a large pot and add salt.

Step 2: Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until al dente.

Step 3: Drain the pasta and return it to the pot, adding olive oil and cheese while mixing well.

Step 4: Serve hot, optionally garnished with herbs or additional cheese.
