Mix Veg Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices to taste (cumin, coriander, garam masala)

Instructions

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.

Step 2: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.

Step 3: Add tomatoes and spices, cook until tomatoes are soft.

Step 4: Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
