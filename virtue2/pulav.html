Pulav Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear, then soak for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and saut√© until fragrant.

Step 3: Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes before adding the soaked rice.

Step 4: Pour in water, add salt, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is fluffy.
