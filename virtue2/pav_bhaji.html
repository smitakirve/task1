Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon pav bhaji masala
  • Butter for serving

Instructions

Step 1: Boil and mash the mixed vegetables.

Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden, then add tomatoes and cook until soft.

Step 3: Add the mashed vegetables and pav bhaji masala, mix well, and cook for a few minutes.
