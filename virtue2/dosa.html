Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

Step 1: Soak rice and urad dal for 4-6 hours.

Step 2: Grind to a smooth batter and let it ferment overnight.

Step 3: Heat a pan, pour the batter, and cook until golden brown.
